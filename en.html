About the concept

What is a mystery scent box?

A mystery scent box is a monthly subscription where you receive a surprise selection of fragrances without knowing in advance what's inside. Instead of spending a fortune blindly on a whole bottle, each month you discover 3-5 mini-size fragrances, from well-known brands to niche creations worth discovering.

Scentry Box is Romania's first scent box dedicated entirely to fragrances, of every kind: personal perfume, room scent for your home, hair mist for your hair and body mist for your skin. And, just like the name says, Scentry is your entry into the world of fragrances.
