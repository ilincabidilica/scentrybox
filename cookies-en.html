Cookie Policy

Last updated: 10.07.2026

We use cookies to make the website work and, only with your consent, to improve it. In accordance with the GDPR and Romanian Law no. 506/2004.

1. What cookies are

They are small files saved by your browser on your device. Some are strictly necessary; the rest (analytics, marketing) are used only if you give your consent.

2. What cookies we use

Analytics and marketing cookies load only after you press “Accept” in the banner. “Decline” is just as easy and does not enable them.

3. How to manage your choice

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time from “Cookie settings” (in the site footer) or from your browser settings.
