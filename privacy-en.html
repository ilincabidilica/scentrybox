Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10.07.2026

Your privacy matters. Below is a short overview of what data we collect through this website and how we use it, in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).

1. Data controller

  • Scentry Box — an independent project, currently in its pre-launch stage.
  • Contact email: contact@scentrybox.ro

2. What data we collect

  • The email address you enter in the sign-up form (optionally, your first name).
  • Technical data via cookies, only with your consent (see the Cookie Policy).

3. Purpose and legal basis

We use your email address to add you to the waitlist and send you news about the Scentry Box launch. The legal basis is your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), which you can withdraw at any time.

4. How long we keep your data

We keep your data until the service launches and for a reasonable period afterwards, but no longer than 24 months from your last interaction, or until you withdraw your consent.

5. Who we share it with

We do not sell your data. We only share it with the providers who help us run the service, acting as processors: our email marketing provider (Mailchimp) and our hosting provider (Cloudflare).

6. Transfers outside the EU/EEA

Some providers may process data outside the EU (e.g. the US). In that case, the transfer is made with appropriate safeguards, such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses or the EU–US Data Privacy Framework.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and to withdraw your consent at any time. You can exercise them by writing to contact@scentrybox.ro, and you can unsubscribe from any email in one click.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority. In Romania this is the ANSPDCP (www.dataprotection.ro).

8. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; the current version is the one marked with the date above.
